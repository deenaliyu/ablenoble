Service: Residential security

 Ablenoblesecurity is able to create a customized security solution specific to your building to protect and secure your building’s residents and assets. 

Our organization approaches residential building security very similarly to commercial building security. As a result of many years of exemplary service, property managers of buildings and home owners associations have trusted our company with the security of their residents. Ablenoblesecurity ’s concierge-style security guards have been trained to provide a white-glove level of service in residential and commercial properties that is unparalleled in the protective services industry. 



Ablenoblesecurity Protection prides itself on providing highly trained security officers that specialize in the following: 


  • Documenting all incidents real-time through our Silvertrac Software
  • Performing regular patrols at designated intervals 
  • Access control and monitoring entries/exits of the premises  
  • Proactively identifying any safety concerns on-site
  • Inspecting safety and security equipment functionality  
  • Monitoring CCTV  

Every one of our security officers is screened and required to complete mandatory training and preparation that is specific to the client’s building needs.
