Service: Construction site

We create and operate the best team possible to provide the highest level of fire guard service to our clients. Our personnel are ready to provide security services to your organization as manned guards for your office or business establishments.

Our company understands the need for security officers when a situation arises, and the need is urgent. Ablenoblesecurity prides itself on the ability to provide our clients with highly trained security guards 24/7, 365 days a year. We make the safety and protection of staff, visitors, and assets our responsibility by providing highly trained security guards that specialize in the following:

  • Unarmed or armed security services
  • Regularly patrolling designated areas
  • Observing and reporting irregular conditions and security concerns
  • Sounding alarms and contacting the police or fire department in case of an emergency
  • Monitoring entries/exits and permitting authorized persons to enter the building
  • Inspecting functionality of security and safety equipment
  • Assisting employees and visitors of the building in a professional manner
  • Providing Retail security services in the event of alarm failure or failure to properly secure the site
  • Documenting all patrol activities in a patrol log

Every one of our security officers is screened and required to complete mandatory training and preparation that is specific to the client’s needs. In addition, our guards are required to regularly check in with our 24/7 dispatch center to make certain that they are active and performing their duties. Our goal is to give our client peace of mind by ensuring the safety and wellbeing of staff, guest, and physical assets.
