About Us

ablenoblesecurity is a 24/7 security guard provider that currently services clients in United States . ablenoblesecurity has been providing armed and unarmed security guard services for retail stores, residential, construction sites, school campuses, and healthcare centers. Our company has established a reputation of protecting industry-leading clients by providing outstanding services. Our security officers are thoroughly vetted, vigorously trained, and fully licensed. Here at ablenoblesecurity , we have established a reputation for excellence and have emerged as a leader in the protection services industry. We provide our clients with customized security plans that are tailored to meet their needs. All our services are provided at competitive rates and are focused on demonstrating the value that our company brings to its clients.

Mission Statement   

ablenoblesecurity ’s mission is to provide clients with highly trained professional security guards that are uniquely capable of protecting their clients, guests, property, and assets, 24/7.

Reliable Armed and Unarmed Security Guards

Our experienced staff can help meet your unique protection service needs no matter what type of security you’re looking for. Whether you want the comforting visual deterrent and welcoming customer service of an unarmed guard, or the reassuring protection of a highly trained professional who is specially licensed to conceal and carry, our guards have you covered.

Unarmed Protection Company

If you’d like to hire someone to watch your property, greet your customers, and report incidents, you will need the services of an unarmed security guard company. Our guards are typically uniformed in suit and tie and trained in hospitality in order to provide a level of professionalism to your business, property, or event.

Unarmed guards are hired for: 

  • Customer service
  • Asset Protection
  • Maintaining a safe and secure environment for customers and staff
  • Access Control
  • Real Time Incident Reporting
  • Contacting emergency services
  • CCTV Monitoring
  • Armed Protection Company
  • In higher risk situations, it may be necessary to hire an armed security guard for your property, business, or event. Armed security guards are highly trained and licensed to conceal and carry firearms. They provide a reassuring security presence and can deescalate potentially dangerous situations should they arise.  Our guards are put through extensive background checks and thoroughly trained in protocols for firearm use.

Armed guards are hired to:

  • Patrol the premises
  • Respond to incidents
  • Protect clients, guests, and property
  • Maintain a safe and secure environment
  • Deter or deescalate potentially violent situations
  • Prevent and deter crime
  • Asset Protection
